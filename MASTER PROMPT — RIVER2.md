# MASTER PROMPT — RIVER2

## Project

Design a premium landing page and design system for a luxury private yacht and boat rental company in Saint Petersburg, Russia.

The goal is to create a website that feels like a luxury hotel, private yacht club or premium travel brand—not a typical boat rental website.

The design should inspire emotion before selling.

The visitor should immediately imagine themselves spending an unforgettable evening on the Neva River.
