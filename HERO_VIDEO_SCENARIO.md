# HERO_VIDEO_SCENARIO.md

# Hero Video Concept (8--9 seconds)

## Goal

Create a cinematic Hero background video that conveys the feeling of a
premium private boat experience in Saint Petersburg. The video should
support the headline **"Experience Saint Petersburg from the Water"**
and encourage visitors to imagine themselves on board.

The video is **not** a city showcase. It is an emotional story about
spending an unforgettable evening on the Neva.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Style

-   Premium
-   Cinematic
-   Calm
-   Elegant
-   Apple / Four Seasons / Aman Resorts aesthetic
-   Slow camera movement
-   Seamless loop

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Technical Requirements

-   Duration: **8--9 seconds**
-   Resolution: source 4K, export Full HD
-   Frame rate: 24--30 FPS
-   Smooth transitions
-   No abrupt cuts
-   Optimized for web playback
-   Seamless looping

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Storyboard

## Scene 1 (0.0--1.3 s)

Drone slowly flies over the Neva.

Petropavlovsk Fortress appears in the distance.

Calm water.

Purpose: Establish scale and atmosphere.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Scene 2 (1.3--2.7 s)

A luxury yacht slowly crosses the frame.

No aggressive movement.

Purpose: The visitor immediately imagines themselves aboard.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Scene 3 (2.7--4.2 s)

Guests enjoy the cruise.

Natural laughter.

Champagne glasses.

Golden sunset.

No posing for the camera.

Purpose: Sell the experience, not the boat.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Scene 4 (4.2--5.8 s)

Drawbridges opening.

Slow cinematic shot.

Purpose: Iconic Saint Petersburg moment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Scene 5 (5.8--7.0 s)

Boat glides beneath a bridge.

Camera close to the water.

Purpose: Immersion and motion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Scene 6 (7.0--8.5 s)

Night Saint Petersburg.

City lights reflected in the river.

Boat continues into the distance.

Fade naturally into Scene 1 for a seamless loop.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Color Palette

Preferred:

-   Golden Hour
-   Blue Hour
-   Evening
-   Warm city lights

Avoid:

-   Flat daylight
-   Overexposed sky
-   Harsh contrast

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Editing Style

Not a fast commercial.

Not an action montage.

Every movement should feel smooth and expensive.

Think:

-   Apple
-   Four Seasons
-   Aman Resorts

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# People

People are essential.

Without people: the yacht looks like a product.

With people: the yacht becomes an experience.

Show:

-   conversations
-   laughter
-   champagne
-   photography
-   relaxed atmosphere

Never:

-   wave at the camera
-   stare into the lens
-   exaggerated posing

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Avoid

-   Fast drone shots
-   Timelapses
-   Quick cuts
-   Crowded tourist scenes
-   Cheap stock footage
-   Artificial smiles

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Visual Balance

Approximately:

-   30% Saint Petersburg
-   70% boating experience

The city provides context.

The boat provides emotion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Idea

The Hero video should tell the story of one perfect evening:

1.  Departure
2.  Sunset
3.  Relaxing on board
4.  Drawbridges opening
5.  Night lights
6.  Sailing into the city

Within eight seconds, the visitor should feel as though they have
already experienced the journey.
